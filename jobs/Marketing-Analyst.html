Marketing Analyst

@ Notearise, Inc.

Brooklyn, NY

At Notearise, we continually look for opportunities to invent, helping users to retain information and be better prepared for life. To that end, we are building a note-taking & preparation product that offers the best user experience.

We are developing a product that bolsters the note-taking strengths of the user and elevates their ability to organize and collaborate. The future of our business is compelling and we are focused on designing world class tools to prepare Notearise users to prepare for it all.

We are building a focused group to analyze current trends in the Productivity industry and identify opportunities to improve our tools. This analysis will not only support the internal Notearise product development team but also be used in published materials like white papers, proposals and ads. The strides Notearise makes over the next few quarters will be - in part - due to the Marketing Analysts efforts.

We are looking for a data driven, analytical Marketing Analyst to join our summer launch team. As a Marketing Analyst, you will be responsible for running quantitative analyses to uncover insights into productivity trends in note-taking and preparation.

What you'll do:

  • This is a project-based internship with the singular goal of performing a competitive analysis of the Productivity app space.
  • Attend daily scrums and share information about marketing trends
  • Create quadrants & scatterplot diagrams

What we're looking for:

  • Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Data Science or related field
  • 1+ year of relevant experience in a business analyst/data analyst/statistical analyst role
  • Advanced working knowledge of spreadsheets (Google Sheets, Apple Numbers, Microsoft Excel, etc.)
  • Advanced working knowledge of MacOS & iOS tools (iPad, iPhone & MacBook, MacBook Pro)
  • Ability to apply statistical techniques (e.g., hypothesis testing)
  • Experience in developing creative strategy for consumer-facing communications
  • Exceptional attention to detail in communications & project management
  • Basic social media skills, including knowledge of industry-standard note-taking apps and web apps
  • Excellent communication (verbal and written) and interpersonal skills and an ability to effectively communicate with both business and technical teams.
  • Proven problem-solving skills
  • Ability to confront ambiguity with goal-driven direction
  • A passionate advocate for Instagram Stories
