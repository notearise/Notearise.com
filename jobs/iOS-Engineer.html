Who We Are:

At Notearise, we continually look for opportunities to invent, helping users to retain information and be better prepared for life. To that end, we are building a note-taking & preparation product that offers the best user experience.

We are developing a product that bolsters the note-taking strengths of the user and elevates their ability to organize and collaborate. The future of our business is compelling and we are focused on designing world class tools to prepare Notearise users to prepare for it all.

Details:

We are building a focused group to analyze current trends in the Productivity industry and identify opportunities to improve our tools.

We are transforming an industry by leveraging mobile technology and leading the way in productivity & note-taking/preparation software arena with a new emphasis on analytics, mobility and digital solutions to help our members reach their goals. One of the first critical steps includes launching a MVP iPad implementation.

If you've got great iOS chops and want to see your work appreciated by thousands of users eager for a new tool, this Notearise iniative is the place for you. We're an agile tech startup intent measured releases focused heavily on user feedback. Starting this Fall, we'll ship every 2 weeks, so there's always something exciting and challenging to work on.

Working with Notearise means you get to take part in reshaping what the future of note-taking looks like. Your work will help thousands be more prepared.
